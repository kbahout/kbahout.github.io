About Me

Hello! My name is Kay, I left Lebanon and moved to Canada three years ago. My favourite season is summer so I really hate the canadian weather. I love cats and fashion, my favourite colors are purple and black. I can fluently speak three languages, Arabic, french and English. I love listening to music especially Fairuz, Haifa Wehbe and Soolking songs.

My hobbies are painting, reading and drawing henna designs. The books that I really enjoyed reading are the Shatter Me series by Tahere Mafi, The Prophet by Gibran Khalil Gibran and The Metamorphosis by F.K. I'm also a Henna designer in my free time and love creating my own drawings.
